    Samriddhi Samarthan Yojana (Prosperity Support Scheme)

    OBC
    18-60

    Details

    The Samriddhi Samarthan Yojana, or the Prosperity Support Scheme, is a government initiative aimed at promoting the socio-economic development of individuals belonging to OBC community. This scheme seeks to uplift these communities by providing financial assistance and access to resources for their holistic growth.

    Eligiblity

    1. The scheme is open to individuals between the ages of 18 and 60.
    2. The scheme is available to individuals with a monthly income below Rs 10,000.
    3. The scheme is applicable to individuals who are unemployed or working in the informal sector.

    Benefits

    1. Eligible individuals will receive 0.5 ETH to support their basic needs.
    2. Participants in the scheme will have access to skill development programs and training opportunities to enhance their employability.
    3. The scheme provides healthcare coverage, including free medical consultations and subsidized medicines, to eligible individuals and their immediate family members.
