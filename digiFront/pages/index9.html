    Kisan Samriddhi Yojana (Farmers' Prosperity Scheme)

    Farmers
    Poor

    Details

    The Kisan Samriddhi Yojana (Farmers' Prosperity Scheme) is a comprehensive initiative aimed at promoting agricultural growth, improving the livelihoods of farmers, and ensuring food security in the nation. It provides a range of support mechanisms to empower farmers and enhance their agricultural practices.

    Eligiblity

    1. Only farmers who own agricultural land are eligible to participate in the scheme.
    2. The scheme is open to farmers belonging from a rural area.
    3. The scheme is targeted towards small and marginal farmers.

    Benefits

    1. Eligible farmers will receive financial assistance to support their agricultural activities, such as purchasing seeds, fertilizers, and machinery.
    2. The scheme provides crop insurance coverage to protect farmers against crop failures due to natural calamities or pests.
    3. Farmers enrolled in the scheme will have access to training programs and educational resources to enhance their farming skills and knowledge.
