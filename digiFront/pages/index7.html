    Shiksha Shakti Yojana (Girl Power Scheme)

    Girls
    16-23

    Details

    The Shiksha Shakti Yojana, also known as the Girl Power Scheme, is a government initiative dedicated to advancing girls' education and empowerment. This scheme aims to break down barriers that hinder girls' access to quality education and create an inclusive learning environment.

    Eligiblity

    1. The scheme is open to adolescent girls between the ages of 16 and 23
    2. Girls applying for the scheme should not have an annual income
    3. Applicants should be permanent residents of India

    Benefits

    1. The scheme focuses on the education and nutrition of adolescent girls. It provides support for their educational expenses and ensures they receive proper nutrition, including iron-folic acid tablets, to improve their overall well-being
    2. It is one of the largest scholarship programs in India.
    3. The scheme aims to empower girls by providing them with knowledge and awareness about nutrition, health, and reproductive health. It also helps raise awareness about the challenges they may face and the preventive measures they can take
