    Government Employee Welfare Fund (GEWF)

    Employees
    21+

    Details

    The Government Employee Welfare Fund (GEWF) is a dedicated initiative to enhance the well-being and overall quality of life for government employees. It provides financial assistance and support to government workers and their families during various life events and emergencies.

    Eligiblity

    1. Employees must have service in the government sector to be eligible for the fund.
    2. Employees with a monthly income below a certain threshold, such as Rs. 50,000, are eligible for the fund.
    3. Employees must be currently employed by the government and not on leave or suspended to be eligible for the fund.

    Benefits

    1. Employees enrolled in the GEWF will receive comprehensive medical insurance coverage for themselves and their immediate family members.
    2. The fund provides financial assistance for the education of employees' children, including scholarships, tuition fee reimbursement, and educational loans.
    3. Upon retirement, employees who have contributed to the GEWF will receive a lump sum amount as a retirement benefit, in addition to their regular pension.
